Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a classic Filipino soup characterized by its sour and savory medley of flavors. It's popular comfort food in the Philippines, usually served on its own or paired with steamed rice on rainy days to ward off the cold.

Ingredients

  • 4¼ cups water
  • 2 cups pork spare rib cut into chunks
  • 1 pc red onion, quartered
  • 2 pcs medium sized tomato, quartered
  • 8 pcs string beans, sliced into 2” pieces
  • 250 g water spinach stalk and leaves
  • 1 pc eggplant, sliced
  • 1 pack Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional
    • 2 pcs banana pepper
    • 1 pc radish, peeled, sliced into ½" thick half-rounds
    • 6 pcs okra, ends trimmed
    • 6 pcs taro, peeled, halved
